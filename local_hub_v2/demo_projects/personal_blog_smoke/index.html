About

A personal blog shaped by warmth, curiosity, and everyday craft.

Lena writes from a sunlit apartment above a corner bakery, collecting observations from train rides, sketchbooks, recipe cards, and neighborhood conversations.

Her blog is part diary, part notebook: a place to share reflections on creative routines, thoughtful travel, and how to build a life that feels both grounded and open.
